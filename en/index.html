Squats Archive

fr en

Squats Archives offers an archive of evicted squats, by city.

The project aims firstly to offer the required data to speak up against those evictions, under the claim of emergency, for projects that never actually occur. We also want to honor all these places and the initiatives they carried. Finally, we hope that this tool may help to have an historical view of the squat culture.

This project is collaborative and autonomous. Squats Archive will present many maps, each maintained by a local collective established in every of those cities.

This project owes everything to it's parent, the London Squat Archive
